### 2. Incident Spawning Logic

**Base Probability**: 5% per check

**Multipliers**:
- Rush hour (7-10 AM, 5-8 PM): **2.5x**
- Weekday: **1.5x**
- Rain: **2.0x**
- Heavy rain: **3.5x**
- Fog: **2.5x**
- Storm: **4.0x**

**Example**: During rush hour in heavy rain on a weekday:
```
Final probability = 0.05 √ó 2.5 √ó 1.5 √ó 3.5 = 0.65625 (66% chance)
```

### 3. Incident Types

**Weather-based**:
- Heavy rain/storm ‚Üí More accidents and weather incidents
- Clear weather ‚Üí More balanced distribution

**Rush hour**:

### 5. Incident Lifecycle

```

   ‚Üì
```
